About Sky Roofing

Maharashtra's Premier Industrial Roofing Manufacturer

Founded in 2013 by Mr. Mushtaq Shah and Mr. Zakir Husain, Sky Roofing has evolved into Maharashtra's most trusted manufacturer, supplier, and trader of premium industrial roofing systems and ventilation solutions.

Operating from our state-of-the-art facility on Ambad Satpur Link Road, Nashik, we serve industrial complexes, MIDC hubs, warehouses, and infrastructure contractors with precision-engineered products backed by over a decade of field expertise.
